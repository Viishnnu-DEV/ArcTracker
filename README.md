# ⚡ Arc House Task Tracker

A privacy-first, local Chrome Extension designed to seamlessly track your completed daily tasks and quotas on [community.arc.io](https://community.arc.io). 

## 🎯 What it does
On Arc House, members earn points by reading content and watching videos up to a rolling 24-hour limit. This extension solves two major frustrations:
1. **Visual Checkmarks:** Automatically injects green checkmarks onto video and article cards in your Home feed so you know what you've already clicked.
2. **Smart Cooldown Timers:** Features a clean dashboard popup that tracks exactly how many tasks you've completed in the last 24 hours, and tells you the exact time your next slot will unlock!

## 🔒 100% Private & Secure
Runs entirely locally on your machine. **Zero network requests.** No analytics, no tracking, and no personal data ever leaves your device. All data is saved safely in `chrome.storage.local`.

## 🚀 How to Install
1. Download this repository as a ZIP and unzip it.
2. Open Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Toggle **Developer mode** ON.
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the folder.
